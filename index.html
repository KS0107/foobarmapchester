Who are we?

What we believe in.

We're a team of first year students who came together to solve one key problem for students, streamlining the process of going out with friends. FooBar lets you create plans with friends with ease and makes the social side of the uni experience much simpler. We're seven first year Computer Science students from the University of Manchester who used this project to improve our HTML, JavaScript, CSS and PHP skills whilst also learning how to collaborate on a large scale web development group project.
